E11.10 Approved work exchange schemes

  1. Approved work exchange schemes allow participants to undertake employment for gain or reward during all or part of their stay.
  2. There are up to 1000 places annually for participants in approved work exchange schemes.
  3. Approved work exchange schemes must operate under the auspices of responsible organisations that have satisfied INZ that appropriate arrangements have been made for the accommodation, maintenance and welfare of the exchange participants and any accompanying dependants.

Effective 29/11/2010
