RW7.15 Evidential requirements

Applications under these instructions must include evidence:

  1. that the applicant has been undertaking religious work in New Zealand for a period of at least three years; and
  2. of work that meets the requirements set out at RW7.5.1; and
  3. of sponsorship by an acceptable sponsor (see RW7.10); and
  4. of at least five years of religious training and/or religious work experience relevant to the religious work the applicant is being sponsored to undertake, that may include, but is not limited to:
    1. testimonials;
    2. certificates of ordination;
    3. curriculum vitae;
    4. documentation demonstrating relevant work experience; or
    5. a verified copy of an awarding certificate for a relevant qualification.

Note: An applicant’s religious training and experience can be considered cumulatively to meet the requirement under (d).
