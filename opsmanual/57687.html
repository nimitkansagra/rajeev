WM4 Making an application under Religious Worker instructions (07/11/2011)

See also Immigration (Visa, Entry Permission, or Related Matters) Regulations 2010 reg 10

Applications under Religious Worker instructions must be made on the INZ form Work Visa Application (INZ 1015), and applicants must provide the following evidence:

  1. training and/or experience relevant to the religious work the applicant is being sponsored to undertake, of at least two years, in the field of religious work (see WM2), including, but not limited to:
    1. testimonials;
    2. certificates of ordination;
    3. curriculum vitae;
    4. documentation demonstrating relevant work experience; or
    5. a verified copy of an awarding certificate for a relevant qualification.
  2. a Sponsorship Form for Religious Workers (INZ 1190) completed by a religious organisation registered as a charity whose purpose is advancing religion;
  3. evidence of good health and character as set out at A4 and A5, if required.

Effective 07/11/2011
