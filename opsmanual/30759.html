BF3 Pre-purchase of English for speakers of other languages (ESOL) tuition

  1. Non-principal applicants who pre-purchase ESOL tuition, instead of meeting the minimum standard of English, must pre-purchase ESOL tuition from the Tertiary Education Commission (TEC) by paying the required charge to INZ (which collects this charge on behalf of the TEC). (See BF3.15.)
  2. Before a resident visa is granted, applicants must pay any ESOL tuition charge due.

Effective 29/11/2010
