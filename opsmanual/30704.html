BA2.5 Temporary class category

The following business immigration categories are part of temporary entry instructions:

  • Entrepreneur work visa (see BB)
  • Global Impact work visa (see BG2)

Persons who are granted a work visa under these categories will have the opportunity to apply for residence. Such persons will need to meet the relevant residence category requirements applying at the time that their residence class visa application is made. Such residence category requirements may differ from those that applied at the time that the person's work visa application was made or at the time the work visa was granted.

Note: People who were granted and still hold a visa under the former Long Term Business Visa Category can also apply for residence under the Entrepreneur Residence Visa Category. Such persons will need to meet the relevant residence category requirements applying at the time that their residence class visa application is made.

Effective 21/11/2016
