WD2 Lodging an application under Post-Study work visa instructions

  1. Applicants must:
    1. lodge their Post-Study work visa application in the prescribed manner (see E4.50); and
    2. provide evidence that they have completed a qualification in New Zealand that meets the requirements as set out at WD3 or WD4; and
    3. provide evidence that they have a minimum of $5,000 in funds available to maintain themselves during their stay in New Zealand.
  2. An offer of employment is not required.

Effective 07/09/2022
