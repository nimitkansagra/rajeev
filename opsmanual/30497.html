RV3.15 Variation of travel conditions for principal applicants with investment conditions under Section 49(1)

See also Immigration Act 2009, ss 49, 50

Principal applicants who have been granted a resident visa under

  1. the Active Investor Migrant policy; or
  2. the Migrant Investment categories; or
  3. the Parent Retirement Category;

may be granted a variation of travel conditions for 24 months if their first day in New Zealand as a resident (see R5.66.5(b)) was at least 24 months before the current application for a variation of travel conditions was made, and they continue to meet any conditions imposed under section 49(1) and/or section 50 of the Immigration Act 2009 unless RV3.1(d) applies.

Effective 29/11/2010
