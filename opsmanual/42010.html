SM17.1 Aim and Intent (29/11/2010)

The aim of providing bonus points for a partner's recognised qualification(s) is to recognise such qualifications as an indicator of the likely contribution of the whole migrating family.

SM17.1.1 Bonus points for a partner's recognised qualifications

  1. Recognised qualifications held by the partner of a principal applicant qualify for 20 points.
  2. A partner's recognised qualfication only qualifies for points under this provision if:
    1. the partner is included in the application; and
    2. the partner meets the English language requirements for principal applicants (see SM5.5); and
    3. an immigration officer is satisfied that the principal applicant and their partner have been living together for 12 months or more in a partnership that is genuine and stable (see F2.10.1) and otherwise meets Partnership Instructions (see F2.15).

Effective 29/11/201
