WD1.5 Relevance of qualification(s) to employment (to 25/11/2018)

Qualifications are relevant to employment if:

  1. the major subject area and level of the principal applicant's recognised qualification is directly applicable to the employment; and
  2. the immigration officer is satisfied that the qualification was a key factor in the employer's decision to employ the principal applicant in that position.

Effective 25/07/2011
