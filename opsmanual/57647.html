R5.60 Date of final decision (29/11/2010)

  1. The date of final decision on an application that is declined is the date when the letter advising that the application has been declined, is posted.
  2. The date of final decision on an application that is approved is the date of grant of the residence class visa.

Effective 29/11/2010
