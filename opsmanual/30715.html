BC4.10 Assessment of a business plan (29/11/2010)

  1. In assessing a business plan, a business immigration specialist will consider the credibility of the information provided and whether the business will benefit New Zealand.
  2. INZ may submit any business plan to an independent person or persons for vetting. They will offer an independent assessment and advice, which will be considered by a business immigration specialist in making a decision.

Effective 29/11/2010
