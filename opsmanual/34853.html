WI3.1 Health and character requirements for personnel working in New Zealand as part of a programme of a Contracting Party to the Antarctic Treaty

A letter from the responsible authority of the Contracting Party to the Antarctic Treaty confirming that the applicant is a person specified at WI3(a) is sufficient evidence that the applicant meets temporary entry class health and character requirements (see A4.5 and A5.5).
