BE2.1 Employee of a relocating business

Principal applicants in the Employees of Relocating Businesses Category are required to demonstrate that:

  1. they are an employee of a relocating business and that they are a key employee; and
  2. the relocation of the business is supported by New Zealand Trade and Enterprise.

Note: An Immigration New Zealand business immigration specialist will consult with New Zealand Trade and Enterprise to determine their support for the relocation of the business (see BE3.1(c)).

Effective 29/11/2010
