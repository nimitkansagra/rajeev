BN7.5 Holding investments (19/09/2022)

  1. A holding investment means investment of funds in:
    1. New Zealand, in New Zealand currency; and
    2. either one or more of the following:
      • bonds issued by the New Zealand government or local authorities; or
      • term deposits with New Zealand registered banks; or
      • deposits in a New Zealand bank account.
  2. Funds invested in a holding investment:
    1. must be in the principal applicant’s name; and
    2. must remain separate from any other funds; and
    3. are not for personal use (see BN7.10.25) or use as a transaction account; and
    4. must not be withdrawn except to place into acceptable investments.

Effective 19/09/2022
