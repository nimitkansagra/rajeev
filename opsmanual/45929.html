WJ6.1 Application requirements

The applicant must:

  1. provide a copy of the Approval in Principle (AIP) letter (if applicable);
  2. meet the standard requirements for a temporary visa (see W2.10);
  3. have at least 12 months’ work experience in a similar position;
  4. have any other required qualifications and experience for the position as specified in the employer’s AIP (or job description for non-AIP applications);
  5. provide an employment agreement, in English and their own language, that meets WJ5.45.10; and
  6. provide the form Sponsorship for Temporary Entry (INZ 1025) completed by the New Zealand employer.

Note: The NZ employer must meet the acceptable sponsor requirements set out at E6.5 and E6.5.1.
