SM5.1 Points

  1. A principal applicant's age qualifies for points as follows:

    Age

    Points

    20-39

    30

    40-44

    20

    45-49

    10

    50-55

    5
  2. Principal applicants aged 56 and over on the date their application is made must be declined under the Skilled Migrant Category.
