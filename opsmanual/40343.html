*If an applicant believes their qualification may be higher than the levels stated above they should apply to the NZQA for an International Qualification Assessment. In the case of Honours degrees, those degrees that include postgraduate study have the possibility of a higher level. Degrees where “Honours” is used as a result of performance are not considered to be postgraduate study.

**Doctorate degrees other than Doctor of Philosophy (PhD or DPhil) degree require a NZQA assessment.
