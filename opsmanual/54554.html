Y6.10 Referrals from Customs officers: not needed if new visa immediately granted

A Customs officer who cancels a person’s visa granted as a result of administrative error:

  1. may, despite Y6.5 (a) above, immediately grant the person a new visa; or otherwise
  2. must refer the person to INZ for consideration under Y6.5.

Note: "Customs officer" means a designated immigration officer under s380 of the Immigration Act 2009.

Effective 02/02/2015
