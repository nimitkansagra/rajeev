F6.30 Undertakings of sponsors (15/12/2010)

  1. Sponsors (except those at F6.30(b) below) must give the mandatory sponsorship undertakings (see R4.10) for the first 2 years from their relatives' first day as a resident in New Zealand.
  2. Sponsors who obtained a residence class visa in New Zealand on the basis of their status as refugees are only required to give an undertaking with respect to the applicant’s accommodation in New Zealand (see R4.10 (b)(i)) for the first 2 years from their relatives' first day as a resident in New Zealand.

Effective 15/12/2010
