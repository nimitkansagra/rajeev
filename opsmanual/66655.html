U4.20 Vocational trainees (08/05/2017)

  1. Only the following applicants may be granted a student visa as a vocational trainee:
    1. Air New Zealand trainees that meet the requirements set out at U6.35.5;
    2. Nautical students that meet the requirements set out at U9.5;
    3. New Zealand Aid Programme Short-Term Training Award (STTA) trainees that meet the requirements set out at U9.15.
  2. Applicants who intend to undertake industry training or a modern apprenticeship, not listed at U4.20(a) above, or U9, must apply for a work visa (see WK).

Effective 08/05/2017
