WR3.10 Inclusion of occupations on the Long Term Skill Shortage List (LTSSL)

  1. The Long Term Skill Shortage List (LTSSL) is a list of occupations in which INZ, in consultation with Industry New Zealand, relevant industry groups and unions, has identified an absolute (sustained and ongoing) shortage of skilled workers.
  2. The Long Term Skill Shortage List is reviewed annually.

WR3.10.1 The Long Term Skill Shortage List

The Long Term Skill Shortage List is held at Appendix 4 of the INZ Operational Manual.
