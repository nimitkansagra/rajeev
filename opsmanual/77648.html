H6.10 Making an application for a relationship-based visitor visa (12/07/2021)

See also Immigration (Visa, Entry Permission, and Related Matters) Regulations 2010, Reg 14A, 26AA

  1. A person may only apply for a visa under these instructions if they are invited to do so.
  2. An application must be made within 4 months of the date of the invitation to apply.
  3. An application for a visa must be made in the prescribed manner.
  4. An application for a relationship-based visitor visa must be made by submitting the following to an immigration officer:
    1. the completed form approved for this purpose; and
    2. the prescribed fee (if any); and
    3. the documentation and evidence requested in the invitation to apply, including evidence of the applicant’s relationship to the New Zealand citizen or resident.

Effective 12/07/2021
