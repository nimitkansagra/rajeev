WI18.10 Evidential requirements (22/08/2016)

To be granted a work visa with study conditions under these instructions, primary sector trainees must:

  1. provide a completed work visa application form, fee and immigration levy; and
  2. provide a letter of support/funding arrangements from their home government; and
  3. provide an offer of place from a high quality education provider (see WI18.5.1 (b)) that is a signatory to the Ministry of Education's Education (Pastoral Care of International Students) Code of Practice 2016; and
  4. agree to hold medical and comprehensive hospitalisation insurance that will remain current throughout their stay in New Zealand.

Effective 22/08/2016
