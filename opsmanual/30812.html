BJ5.25 Age (to 28/07/2022)
  1. Principal applicants under the Investor 2 Category must be aged 65 years or younger at the time of application.
  2. A principal applicant's age under the Investor 2 Category qualifies for points as follows:

    Age

    Points

    60 - 65

    0

    50 - 59

    5

    40 - 49

    10

    30 - 39

    15

    Less than 30

    20

BJ5.25.1 Evidence of age

Evidence of age may include, but is not limited to, original or certified copies of:

  1. a birth certificate; or
  2. a passport or other travel document; or
  3. an identity document (from countries which require these and where birth details are confirmed before the document is issued).

Effective 19/06/2017
