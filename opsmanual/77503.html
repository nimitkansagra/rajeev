A15.25 Delegation of powers: Deputy Secretary to delegates (09/02/2022)

Instrument of Sub-delegation

Delegation by

The holder for the time being of the office of the Deputy Chief Executive of Immigration New Zealand ("Delegator")

Delegation to

Listed in Schedule One ("Delegate")

Source of delegated powers and functions

Listed in Schedule One

Authority for Delegation

Public Service Act 2020 ("PSA", schedule 6, clause 2)
  1. I, the Delegator, delegate to you, the Delegate, the powers and functions specified in the Schedule.
  2. The powers and functions may be exercised subject to any general or specific conditions I may give from time to time. General conditions may be included in any policy on statutory delegations put in place by the Ministry from time to time.
  3. For the avoidance of doubt, the powers and functions listed in Schedule One may not be sub-delegated.
  4. For the avoidance of doubt, a delegation to a specified position also includes senior ranks of that position (for example Compliance Officers also means Senior Compliance Officers).
  5. This delegation is intended to be a standing sub-delegation –
    1. From any person holding the office of, or acting as, the Delegator (PSA, schedule 6, clause 4); and
    2. To any person holding the office of, or acting as, the Delegate (PSA, schedule 6, clause 2(14)).
  6. Paragraph 5(b) above does not apply to the delegation to the General Manager Operations, Associate Deputy Chief Executive. The delegation to the General Manager Operations, Associate Deputy Chief Executive is to the person holding that office, but is not to a person acting in that role.
  7. This sub-delegation applies in respect of the powers and functions in Schedule One, as those powers and functions are amended from time to time.
  8. I revoke any previous sub-delegations by the holder of the office of Deputy Chief Executive of Immigration New Zealand of powers and functions under the provisions of the enactments described and referenced in the Schedule to this Instrument where they have been amended or removed from these Schedules (PSA, schedule 6, clause 4). All other current and previous delegations of powers and functions under the provisions of the enactments described and referenced in the Schedules to this Instrument are confirmed.

Dated at Wellington this 9th day of February 2022

Alison McDonald

Deputy Chief Executive Immigration, Immigration New Zealand
