U7.10 Student visa holders liable for deportation (02/12/2013)

See also Immigration Act 2009 ss 49, 52, 56, 157

  1. A student visa holder who breaches any of the conditions of their visa (in particular those relating to attendance, employment, holding acceptable insurance and living with their legal guardian) may become liable for deportation (see E3.60 and U13.1c).
  2. Where the student is required to live with a legal guardian (see U3.30) and that legal guardian becomes liable for deportation, the student will also become liable for deportation (see E3.60).

Effective 02/12/2013
