Y4.40 People who may be refused entry permission: not supplying biometric information (02/02/2015)

See also Immigration Act 2009 s 111

  1. A person who applies for entry permission must allow biometric information to be collected from him or her if requested to do so by an immigration officer.
  2. An immigration officer may refuse to grant the person entry permission if the person fails to allow the biometric information to be collected.

Effective 02/02/2015
