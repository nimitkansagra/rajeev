RW2.1 Eligibility for a permanent resident visa

A permanent resident visa may be granted to an applicant under Talent (Accredited Employers) work instructions who:

  1. meets all the requirements to be granted a residence class visa; and
  2. at the time of their application for a residence class visa, has employment in New Zealand with a minimum base salary of NZ$90,000; and
  3. made the application for the associated Talent (Accredited Employers) work visa before 7 October 2019.

Effective 07/10/2019
