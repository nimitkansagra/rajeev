WS1 Objective

The objective of the Specific Purpose or Event Instructions is to facilitate entry to New Zealand for a specific purpose or event for which the applicant has demonstrated skills, expertise or attributes that are likely to benefit individuals and/or New Zealand and where there is no risk of a negative impact on opportunities for New Zealand citizens or residents.

Effective 08/05/2017
