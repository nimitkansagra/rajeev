SM4.15 English language requirements for partners where bonus points are claimed for the partner's skilled employment or recognised qualifications

A partner's skilled employment in New Zealand (see SM9.5) or recognised qualifications (see SM9.10), only qualify for points if the partner meets the English language requirements for principal applicants (see SM4.5).
