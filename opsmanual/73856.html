WK2.5 Lodging a request for approval in principle to recruit overseas workers (28/08/2017)
  1. Applications for approvals in principle must be made on the form ‘Request for Approval in Principle’ (INZ 1112).
  2. A request for approval in principle must be accompanied by evidence that:
    1. the employment is acceptable (WK3.5); and
    2. there are no New Zealand citizens or residents available for the work (WK3.10); and
    3. the employer meets requirements (WK3.15).
  3. To determine the skill-band of the proposed employment, evidence must be provided of:
    1. the proposed remuneration; and
    2. the hours of work, or of the arrangements relating to the number of hours to be worked.

Effective 28/08/2017
