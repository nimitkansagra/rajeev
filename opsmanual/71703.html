U14 Pathway student visas pilot (21/05/2018)

  1. For the purposes of these instructions, a pathway is defined as a progression of up to three consecutive programmes of study, offered by qualifying education providers, either within their institution or in conjunction with other qualifying education providers.
  2. Pathway study can be undertaken on a single student visa.
  3. Any application for a pathway student visa made after 30 November 2018 must be declined.

Effective 21/05/2018
