SR2.1 Skilled Residence: Straight to Residence objective (05/09/2022)

The objective of the Skilled Residence: Straight to Residence sub-category is to provide for the grant of resident visas to people who demonstrate they have skills to fill specified high skilled or hard to fill and high national importance roles in New Zealand.

Effective 05/09/2022
