A15.10 Instrument of Delegation September 2011

Delegation of Powers to Department of Labour - Immigration Group

  1. PURSUANT to section 380 of the Immigration Act 2009 I, Jonathan David Coleman, Minister of Immigration, hereby DELEGATE the power in paragraph (2) to each immigration officer who is the holder (or is acting as the holder) from time to time of a position set out in:
    1. Schedule 1 and 2 of the Instrument of Delegation 2011, dated 5th April 2011
    2. Schedule 3 of the Instrument of Delegation 2011, dated 5th April 2011 and who has had this power assigned to them by the Chief Executive.
  2. The delegated power is the power under s50(3) of the Immigration Act 2009 to do either of the things in subsection (2)(b), that is to vary or cancel conditions on a resident visa, by agreement with the visa holder.
  3. Any person who exercises any power pursuant to this Instrument of Delegation shall do so in accordance with the Government policy and Departmental instructions that are current at the time, as set out from time to time in the Immigration New Zealand Operational Manual and circular instructions on Immigration.

Dated at Wellington this 7th day of September 2011

Hon Dr Jonathan David Coleman

Minister of Immigration
