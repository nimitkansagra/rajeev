U15 Multiple short-term English language programmes of study

  1. Applicants may be granted a student visa to undertake multiple short–term English language programmes of study for a maximum period of six months if they:
    1. submit a joint covering letter from eligible pathway education providers (U14.20.1) setting out the English language programmes of study and start and end dates of each programme of study showing at least 14 weeks of English language study in total;
    2. submit an offer of place (or joint offer) for each programme of study that meets the requirements set out at U3.5;
    3. meet the general requirements to be granted a student visa as set out at U3.1;
    4. can demonstrate they have sufficient funds for maintenance for the duration of their stay in New Zealand (U3.20);
    5. have not previously been granted a visa under these instructions.
  2. Work conditions will not be granted unless the part –time work requirements set out at U13.15.1 (e) are met.

Effective 07/12/2015
