WJ2.5 Sound financial position evidential requirements

Evidence that an employer is in a sound financial position includes, but is not limited to:

  1. a statement from a chartered accountant confirming the business is financially sound and is able to meet all outstanding obligations;
  2. an authenticated set of accounts showing a sound financial position;
  3. annual reports;
  4. business plan.
