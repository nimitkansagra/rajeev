Diplom / Diplom eines Wissenschaftszweiges

The award certificate must include the heading ‘Diplom’.

The qualification title ‘Diplom’ will also include a subject specialty. For example, for the subject specialties mathematics or economics, the qualification title may be presented in any of the following formats:

  • Diplom – mathematiker
  • Diplomwirtschaftler.

Diplom

The award certificate may include the heading of ‘Diplom’ or ‘Diplomurkunde’, or ‘Verleiht’.

The qualification title ‘Diplom’ will also include a subject specialty. For example, for the subject specialty physics, the qualification title may be presented in any of the following formats:

  • Diplom–Physiker, or

Doktor / Doktor eines Wissenschaftzweiges

The qualification title ‘Doktor’ will include a subject specialty. For example, for the specialties engineering or natural sciences, the qualification title may be presented in any of the following formats:

  • Doktor – Ingenieur (Dr. -Ing), or
  • Doktor der Naturwissenschaften (Dr. rer. nat).

Doktor der / Doktorin der

The award certificate may include the heading of ‘Promotionsurkunde.’

The qualification title will include the title ‘Doktor’, ‘Doktors’ or Doktorin’ and a subject specialty. For example, for the speciality natural sciences, the qualification title may be presented in any of the following formats:

  • Doktor der Naturwissenschaften (Dr. rer. nat.), or
  • Doktors der Naturwissenschaften (Dr. rer. nat.), or
  • Doktorin der Naturwissenschaften (Dr. rer. nat.).

* Abbreviations used are for the purposes of this list and may not be stated on the award certificates.

Note:
- Award certificates must be provided in their original language for all qualifications.
- All honorary degrees must be referred to the NZQA for an International Qualification Assessment.
- If any courses leading to the award of one of the qualifications listed above were taken outside of Germany, including courses which have been cross-credited towards the listed qualifications please refer to the NZQA for an International Qualification Assessment.
- All qualifications awarded after 2011 should be referred to NZQA for an International Qualification Assessment unless an awarding date after 2011 is specifically listed.
