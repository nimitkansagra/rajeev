United Kingdom exceptions (25/07/2011)

United Kingdom Exceptions

The following qualifications qualify for the points indicated where:

  • the qualification is relevant to an occupation in Part B of the List of Skilled Occupations at Appendix 6; and
  • the applicant has completed an apprenticeship relevant to the qualification, registered with an applicable awarding body specified below; and
  • the qualification was awarded by an applicable awarding body specified below.
