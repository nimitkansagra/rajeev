BJ8.15 Section 49(1) condition: minimum period of time in New Zealand (25/07/2011)

As set out at BJ8.10(a), the principal applicant under each category of the Migrant Investment Categories must spend a minimum period of time in New Zealand during the required investment period. The time periods are:

  1. Investor 1 Category – 12 percent of each year (44 days per year).
  2. Investor 2 Category – 40 percent of each year (146 days per year).

Effective 25/07/2011
