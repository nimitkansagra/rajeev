WI17.20 Indonesian Bahasa Teachers’ Aides

These instructions allow up to 20 Indonesian Bahasa Teachers’ Aides at any one time to work in New Zealand for a period of up to three years provided that they:

  1. have a full-time job offer from a New Zealand employer as a Bahasa Teachers’ Aide, which has been assessed as equivalent to at least ANZSCO Skill Level 4; and
  2. hold a relevant qualification and/or have relevant work experience, as set out for this occupation in ANZSCO; and
  3. meet the requirements set out in WI17.5.

Effective 26/11/2012
