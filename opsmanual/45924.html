WJ5.35 Responsibility to keep and make available accurate employment records

  1. The employer must keep accurate, up-to-date records of:
    1. hours worked by each foreign crew member;
    2. deductions made from each foreign crew member’s earnings;
    3. what payment is actually received by each foreign crew member and where and when payment is received; and
    4. the exchange rate used where payment is in foreign currency.
  2. The employer must provide those records, as they relate to individual crew members, to that crew member or their authorised representative:
    1. when making any wage payments in cash, for the period to which the payment corresponds, and
    2. at any other time if requested by the crew member.

Effective 17/12/2012
