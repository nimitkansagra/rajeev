V1 Objective

  1. The objective of visitor visa instructions is to facilitate the movement of visitors to New Zealand while minimising the risks to New Zealand, and in particular;
  2. To foster tourism, trade and commerce, international understanding and cross-cultural links, educational and scientific activities, while:
    1. maintaining the health, safety and good order of New Zealand society; and
    2. protecting New Zealand from international crime, terrorism and illegal immigration.

Effective 29/11/2010
