Germany – Technical Vocational Qualifications

A qualification is exempt from assessment and qualifies for the indicated points if:

  1. the qualification is listed in the Vocational Qualifications Table; and
  2. the awarding institution is listed in the Berufsakademien Table; and
  3. the qualification was awarded within the year range listed in the right-hand column in the Berufsakademien Table; and
  4. the qualification is not excluded by the notes listed under the Vocational Qualifications Table.
