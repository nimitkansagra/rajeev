BN6.20 Evidence of the nominated funds and/or assets

  1. The principal applicant must provide evidence that:
    1. demonstrates ownership of the nominated funds and/or assets, and that the nominated funds and/or assets were legally earned or acquired; and
    2. the funds and/or assets nominated are equivalent in value to NZ$15 million or weighted equivalent (see BN7.1).
  2. All documents provided as valuations of assets must be:
    1. no more than three months old at the date the resident visa application is made; and
    2. produced by an external and reputable agency; and
    3. credible, as determined by a business immigration specialist.
  3. A business immigration specialist may seek further evidence if they:
    1. are not satisfied that the nominated funds and/or assets were legally earned or acquired; or
    2. consider that the nominated funds and/or assets may have been gifted without being declared or borrowed; or
    3. are not satisfied with the valuation provided; or
    4. consider that he nominated funds and/or assets do not meet the requirements in these instructions.
