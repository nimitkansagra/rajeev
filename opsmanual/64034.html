BG2.15 Currency and conditions of Global Impact work visas

  1. Successful applicants for a Global Impact work visa will be granted a work visa allowing:
    1. 36 months stay and multiple entry travel conditions, if the applicant is in New Zealand; or
    2. three months to enter New Zealand, 36 months stay and multiple travel conditions to be granted on arrival, if the applicant is outside New Zealand.
  2. Global Impact work visas will be granted with the following conditions:
    1. the holder may work in any employment, including self-employment, in any location in New Zealand; and
    2. the holder must retain the support of the provider throughout the duration of their visa.

Effective 21/11/2016
