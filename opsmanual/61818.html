RV4.15 Conditions imposed on a second or subsequent resident visa (07/11/2011)

  1. Any conditions (except travel conditions) imposed under section 49(1) of the Immigration Act 2009 on the expired visa must be replicated on any second or subsequent resident visa granted. Any such conditions must be valid until the same date as on the applicant’s previous resident visa.
  2. Multiple entry travel conditions granted on second or subsequent resident visa must be valid for the longest of:
    1. the date the variation of travel conditions would have been valid to, had the principal applicant applied for one on the date the current applicant’s resident visa expired; or
    2. 24 months from the date the current applicant’s resident visa expired if the principal applicant was eligible for a permanent resident visa on that day; or
    3. the duration specified by special provisions for the grant of a second or subsequent resident visa set out at RV4.20.

Effective 07/11/2011
