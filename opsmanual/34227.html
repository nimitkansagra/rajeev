U3.5 Offer of a place (29/11/2010)

Applicants must have an offer of a place with a New Zealand education provider, which shows:

  1. the name of the course and the minimum time required for completing it; and
  2. evidence that the course and education provider meet foreign student requirements (see U5); and
  3. the amount of the fee for the complete course, or, if the course is longer than one year, the annual fee; and
  4. whether or not the student has to pay course fees and whether the fees are domestic or foreign fees; and
  5. whether the student is full-time or part-time.
