U3.15 Accommodation requirements for fee-paying foreign students

Fee-paying foreign students who are under the age of 18 must provide, with their student visa application, a statement from their education provider confirming that the student’s accommodation is compliant with the Education (Pastoral Care of Tertiary and International Learners) Code of Practice 2021.

Effective 31/07/2022
