WJ4.5 Employer (to 08/05/2017)

Note: These instructions cease to be effective from 8 May 2017.

  1. For the purpose of Immigration Instructions on Foreign Crew of Fishing Vessels, “Employer” is defined as the person or company with the direct employment relationship with foreign crew.
  2. Where the employer is not a New Zealand company, the New Zealand Charter Party (NZCP) with which the employer has a Charter Agreement is responsible for monitoring the employer’s compliance with the relevant Immigration Instructions.

Effective 17/12/2012
