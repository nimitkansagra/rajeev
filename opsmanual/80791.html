WA4.1 Overview (04/07/2022)

  1. The Accredited Employer work visa instructions provide for the grant of work visas to non-New Zealand citizen or resident workers with an offer of employment to work in New Zealand for an accredited employer.
  2. The minimum guaranteed remuneration for the employment determines the ability of an Accredited Employer work visa holder to support temporary visas for family members (WA4.10.10, WF3.1 and V3.10).

Effective 04/07/2022
