WS5 Conditions of specific purpose or event work visas (to 08/05/2017)

Note: These instructions cease to be effective from 8 May 2017.

  1. Work visas granted to applicants approved under these instructions will be endorsed with conditions appropriate to the needs of the applicant in undertaking their specific purpose or event.

    Example: A work visa for an applicant applying as an entertainer should be endorsed as follows:
    i As Musician (Violinist)
    ii For New Zealand Symphony Orchestra
    iii At New Zealand

Effective 29/11/2010
