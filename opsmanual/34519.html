WL3.5 Currency and conditions of a Silver Fern Practical Experience visa (29/11/2010)

  1. Silver Fern Practical Experience visas must be granted with the following conditions imposed by special direction:
    1. the holder may work in the occupation specified on their job offer;
    2. the holder may work for the employer specified on their job offer;
    3. the holder may work at the location specified on their job offer.
  2. A Silver Fern Practical Experience visa may be granted for the period for which employment is offered, up to a maximum of 2 years.
  3. Despite (b) above, the duration of any second or subsequent Silver Fern Practical Experience visa cannot be greater than 2 years from the date the original visa was granted (i.e. the total time spent on Silver Fern Practical Experience visas cannot exceed 2 years).
  4. Upon application, a multiple entry work visa may be issued with the same conditions and for the same duration as a work permit granted under these instructions.

Effective 29/11/2010
