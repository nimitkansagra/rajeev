United Kingdom - Trade and Craft Qualifications (26/09/2011)

Trade Qualifications

The qualifications in Table A qualify for the points indicated where:

  • the qualification is relevant to an occupation in Part B of the List of Skilled Occupations at Appendix 6; and
  • the qualification is awarded by an institution specified in Table A; and
  • the applicant provides evidence of completing:
    • a National Vocational Qualification (NVQ) or a Scottish Vocational Qualification (SVQ) at either Level 2 or Level 3; and
    • a National Certificate awarded by BTEC or Edexcel; or a Certificate awarded by City & Guilds of London Institute

which are relevant to the specific qualification included in Table A.

Level 3 Qualifications

Appendix 3 does not include Level 3 qualifications. Level three qualifications which are recognised as an exception under the Skilled Migrant Category are included in the list of Qualifications recognised as an Exception (Appendix 8).

Craft Qualifications

The craft qualifications in Table C qualify for the points indicated where:

  • the qualification is awarded by the City & Guilds of London Institute in England, Northern Ireland, Scotland or Wales; and
  • the qualification is relevant to an occupation in Part B of the List of Skilled Occupations at Appendix 6; and
  • the applicant provides evidence of completing a time-based apprenticeship which is included in Table D; and
  • the time-based apprenticeship is in an occupation that is relevant to the awarded qualification’s major subject area; and
  • the time-based apprenticeship is awarded by a body listed in Table E.
