SM3.10 Submission of Expressions of Interest to the Pool (to 27/08/2017)

Note: This instruction cease to be effective from 28/08/2017

See also Immigration (Visa, Entry Permission, and Related Matters) Regulations 2010, reg 9

Expressions of Interest (EOIs) submitted in the prescribed manner may be entered into a Pool of Expressions of Interest (the Pool) if the person expressing interest:

  1. has confirmed that health and character requirements for entry to the Pool have been met because none of the people included in their EOI are people who:
    1. are described in sections 15 or 16 of the Immigration Act 2009 (see A5.20); or
    2. would not be granted a medical waiver (see A4.60).
  2. has confirmed that they meet the minimum standard of English (see SM5); and
  3. has claimed a minimum of 100 points for employability and capacity building factors (see SM7 to SM19); and
  4. has claimed points for either recognised qualifications (see SM14) or work experience (see SM11); and
  5. is aged 55 years or younger.

Effective 30/07/2012
