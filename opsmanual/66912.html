United Kingdom – Other Trade Qualifications

The trade qualifications in Table A qualify for the points indicated where:

  • the qualification is awarded by the City and Guilds of London Institute in England, Northern Ireland, Scotland or Wales between 1990-2004; and
  • the qualification is relevant to an occupation at ANZSCO skill level 3, 4 or 5; and
  • the applicant provides evidence of completing a time-based apprenticeship (refer to Table B) awarded by an appropriate awarding body, in a relevant occupation.
