If an applicant believes their qualification may be higher than the level stated they should apply to the NZQA for an International Qualification Assessment.

Note: Award certificates must be provided in their original language for all qualifications. Some universities may award qualification certificates in Latin or Filipino, in which case, a translated copy from the university, or an official translated copy, must also be provided.

All honorary degrees must be referred to the NZQA for an International Qualification Assessment.

If any courses leading to the award of one of the qualifications listed above were studied at any institution outside of the Philippines, including any Philippines overseas campuses, or any courses which have been cross-credited towards the listed qualifications, please refer the qualification to the NZQA for an International Qualification Assessment.

All qualifications awarded after 2013 should be referred to NZQA for an International Qualification Assessment.
