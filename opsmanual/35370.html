Y2.45 Persons returning to New Zealand through emergency

See also Immigration Act 2009 s 122

The holder of a temporary entry class visa, upon application, must be granted entry permission and a further temporary visa current for at least 14 days on their return if, on leaving New Zealand for another country:

  1. the craft is forced to return to New Zealand because of an emergency or circumstances beyond the person’s control; and
  2. their visa has expired or is due to expire at any time between their leaving New Zealand and 14 days after their return to New Zealand.

Note: Y2.45 applies unless the person is subject to sections 15 or 16 of the Immigration Act 2009.
