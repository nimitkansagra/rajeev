WA2.15 Requirements for Standard Accreditation

In addition to meeting the requirements for all employers set out at WA2.10 and WA2.30, employers applying for Standard Accreditation must have no more than 5 jobs associated to them (WA3.25 and WA3.50.1) at any one time.

Effective 23/05/2022
