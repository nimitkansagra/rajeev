BJ5.1 Ability to apply (29/11/2010)

See also Immigration Act 2009 ss 57, 94

A person may only apply for a resident visa under the Investor 2 Category if:

  1. they have been issued with an Invitation to Apply (ITA) under the Investor 2 Category; and
  2. they apply for a resident visa under the Investor 2 Category within four months of the date of the letter in which that invitation is made; and
  3. that ITA has not been revoked.

Effective 29/11/2010
