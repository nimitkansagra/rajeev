WK2.1 Lodging an Essential Skills work visa application (13/05/2019)
  1. Applications for Essential Skills work visas must be lodged in the prescribed manner (see E4.50) and include an Employer Supplementary Form (INZ 1113) completed by the employer.
  2. Applicants who hold a work visa with remuneration as a condition of that visa (see WK4.5(d)), must include evidence of their remuneration payment, such as Inland Revenue income summaries and bank statements.
  3. Applicants must provide:
    1. a copy of the proposed employment agreement; and
    2. a copy of the signed offer of employment (see W2.10.10).
  4. Applications made on the basis of an offer of employment in an ANZSCO skill level 4 or 5 occupation must include a valid Skills Match Report prepared by Work and Income, unless:
    1. the employer holds valid approval in principle for the role identified; or
    2. the role is on an Essential Skills in Demand list and the applicant meets the qualification and/or experience requirements; or
    3. Work and Income have advised Immigration New Zealand of a regional absolute labour shortage (WK3.10.1 (e)(i)); or
    4. the role is included in a list of occupations published by Work and Income that are exempt from the Skills Match Report process, and meets any additional requirements of that list (e.g. region of employment).

Note: A Skills Match Report may be used to support more than one work visa application, as long as it remains valid. A copy of the Skills Match Report should be included with each visa application.

Effective 13/05/2019
