SM6.60 Bonus points for high remuneration (15/01/2018)

  1. Recognised current skilled employment or an offer of skilled employment qualifies for 20 bonus points if the remuneration for that employment is $48.58 per hour or above (or the equivalent annual salary); and
  2. the assessing officer is satisfied that the employment is genuine; and
  3. the assessing officer is satisfied that the employment is sustainable by the employer at the specified level of remuneration (see SM6.30.10).

Effective 15/01/2018
