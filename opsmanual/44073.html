WS6.5 Referral to the relevant New Zealand performers’ union or professional association (to 08/05/2017)

Note: These instructions cease to be effective from 8 May 2017.

The employer must obtain agreement of the relevant New Zealand performers’ union or professional association as set out at WS6.10 unless an applicant’s engagement in New Zealand:

  1. is 14 days or less; or
  2. will be undertaken on an official co-production; or
  3. is with a company that holds entertainment industry accreditation (see WS6.15).

Effective 30/04/2012
