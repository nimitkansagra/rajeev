A20.10 Who may obtain an endorsement

See also Immigration Act 2009 s 13(4)(b)

  1. New Zealand citizens can not hold a visa, however New Zealand citizens who wish to enter New Zealand as a New Zealand citizen on a foreign passport may obtain an endorsement.
  2. Despite A20.10(a) above, a New Zealand citizen who is a national of one or more other countries and is not travelling on a New Zealand passport may hold a visa subject to meeting the relevant immigration instructions if she or he has not been:
    1. granted New Zealand citizenship; or
    2. registered as a New Zealand citizen by descent under section 7(2) of the Citizenship Act 1977; or
    3. issued with an evidentiary certificate under section 21 of the Citizenship Act 1977 confirming that he or she is a New Zealand citizen.

Effective 29/11/2010
