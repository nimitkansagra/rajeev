United Kingdom - Technical and Vocational Qualifications

A qualification is exempt from assessment and qualifies for the indicated points if:

  1. the qualification is listed in the Technical and Vocational Qualifications Table; and
  2. the awarding body is listed in the Awarding Bodies Table; and
  3. the qualification abbreviation is listed in the right-hand column in the Awarding Bodies Table; and
  4. the qualification was awarded from or within the year range listed in the right-hand column in the Awarding Bodies Table.
  5. the qualification is not excluded by the notes listed under the Technical and Vocational Qualifications Table.

If an applicant believes their qualification may be higher than the level stated they should apply to NZQA for an International Qualification Assessment.

Note:
- Award certificates must be provided in their original language.

The following must be referred to NZQA for an International Qualifications Assessment:
- All listed qualifications where study was completed at any institution outside of the country, including any overseas campuses of any listed awarding institution
- All listed qualifications that contain cross-credited courses or papers
- All qualifications awarded after 2017
