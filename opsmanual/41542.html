Zimbabwe exceptions (25/07/2011)

The following qualification qualifies for the points indicated where:

  • the qualification is relevant to an occupation in Part B of the List of Skilled Occupations at Appendix 6;
  • the applicant has completed a Contract of Apprenticeship, relevant to the qualification, registered with a recognised industry body; and
  • the applicant has passed their associated trade test.
