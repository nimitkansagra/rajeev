BH2.1 Successful establishment of a business that is benefiting New Zealand (29/11/2010)

Principal applicants in the Entrepreneur Category are required to demonstrate that:

  1. they have established a business in New Zealand; and
  2. the business is benefiting New Zealand.

Effective 29/11/2010
