RA8 Resident visas with conditions

See also Immigration Act 2009 ss 49, 55, 50

  1. An immigration officer may impose conditions under sections 49 or 55 of the Immigration Act 2009 on a resident visa as specified in residence instructions at the time the application for the visa was made.
  2. Regardless of whether or not any conditions are imposed under sections 49 or 55, under section 50 the Minister or appropriately delegated immigration officer may
    1. impose conditions in addition to those specified in the applicable residence instructions (if any);
    2. vary or waive conditions that would otherwise apply to a visa of that type;
    3. impose, by special direction or by agreement of the visa holder, further conditions following the grant of a resident visa;
    4. vary or cancel, by special direction or by agreement of the visa holder, any conditions that would otherwise apply.

Effective 29/11/2010
