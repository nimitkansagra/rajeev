Germany - Craft and Trade Qualifications (28/02/2011)

Craft and Trade Qualifications Awarded in the Federal Republic of Germany and the former German Democratic Republic

Level 3 Qualifications

Level 3 trade qualifications, included in the list held at Appendix 8, are recognised for the award of points as an exception.

Craft and Trade Qualification

The following craft and trade qualification qualifies for the points indicated where the qualification is relevant to an occupation in Part B of the List of Skilled Occupations at Appendix 6, and was awarded by one of the Chambers listed below.

Qualifications

Level in New Zealand terms

Points under SMC

Meisterprüfung (Meisterbrief, Meisterpreis)

At least 4*

50

*If an applicant believes their qualification may be higher than level 4 they should apply to the NZQA for a qualification assessment report.
