South Pacific Countries (14/05/2012)

List of Campuses of the University of the South Pacific

The University of the South Pacific (established in 1968) is a regional university with Campuses in 12 Countries: Fiji, Cook Islands, Kiribati, Nauru, Niue, Solomon Islands, Tokelau, Tonga, Tuvalu, Vanuatu, Independent State of Samoa, and the Marshall Islands.

Note:

- If any courses leading to the award of one of the qualifications listed above were taken outside of the countries stated, including courses which have been cross-credited towards the listed qualifications please refer to the NZQA for an International Qualification Assessment.
- All qualifications awarded after 2011 should be referred to NZQA for an International Qualification Assessment unless an awarding date after 2011 is specifically listed.
