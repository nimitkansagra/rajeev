BF3.15 The amount of ESOL tuition to be pre-purchased by non-principal applicants (26/11/2012)

  1. The amount of ESOL tuition to be pre-purchased is determined by the applicant's average IELTS score across all four bands (as shown in their "Overall Band" score in the IELTS Test Report Form) according to the following table.

    Overall Band score

    Charge to be paid

    ESOL entitlement

    4.5 or more but less than 5

    NZ$1,735

    NZ$1,531.82

    4 or more, but less than 4.5

    NZ$3,420

    NZ$3,063.64

    3.5 or more, but less than 4

    NZ$5,110

    NZ$4,600.00

    Less than 3.5

    NZ$6,795

    NZ$6,131.82

    Note: No ESOL tuition is required to be pre-purchased if the applicant meets the English language requirements for the category they are applying under.

  2. The charge includes the applicant's ESOL tuition entitlement, as well as the INZ and TEC administration costs.
  3. If an applicant has not submitted IELTS results when requested, the maximum charge of NZ$6,795 applies.

Effective 26/11/2012
