E2.80 Ban on the grant of work visas to Fijian citizens for the purpose of taking up work of a temporary seasonal nature (29/11/2010)

Despite the provisions of work instructions (see WH1.1), applications made for work visas for the purposes of work of a temporary seasonal nature by citizens of Fiji will be declined.

Effective 29/11/2010
