Skilled Migrant Category Resident Visa

The following information is for people who are applying for the Skilled Migrant Category Resident Visa under the new criteria, on or after 9 October 2023.

Use the following link to find the Australian and New Zealand Standard Classification of Occupations (ANZSCO) code and skill level that best matches your job or job offer.

We use version 1.3 of the ANZSCO for Skilled Migrant Category Resident Visa applications under the new visa criteria.

Version 1.3 of ANZSCO - Statistics New Zealand

Occupations that can be recognised at a higher skill level

There are 6 occupations that can be recognised at a higher skill level. These occupations can be recognised at skill level 3 or higher if you meet certain requirements.

To meet these requirements you must have either:

  • 3 years or more of work experience relevant to your job
  • an occupational registration for your job, or
  • a qualification that is relevant to your job, that is at least level 4 (for ANZSCO skill level 3) or a diploma (for ANSZCO skill level 2) on the New Zealand Qualifications and Credentials Framework (NZQCF).

The 6 occupations that can be recognised at a higher skill level are:

  • 423313 Personal Care Assistant (Skill Level 4)
  • 591212 Import-Export Clerk (Skill Level 4)
  • 423111 Aged or Disabled Carer (Skill Level 4)
  • 423312 Nursing Support Worker (Skill Level 4)
  • 712211 Driller (Skill Level 4)
  • 899911 Bicycle Mechanic (Skill Level 5)

Alert

ANZSCO has been updated and some occupations that were skill level 4 or 5 are now skill level 1 to 3 in the updated version. If you are paid at least NZD $29.66 an hour for one of these occupations, we will treat it as an exception and assess it as ANZSCO skill level 1, 2 or 3.

List of ANZSCO occupations treated as an exception

Dairy Cattle Farmer occupations

Applications made from 15 February 2021, for Dairy Cattle Farmer occupations with the ANZSCO code 121313 are assessed on version 1.3 of the ANZSCO.  This includes:

  • Dairy Farm Manager (Skill Level 1)
  • Assistant Dairy Farm Manager (Skill Level 3)
  • Dairy Herd Manager (Skill Level 3)

Definitions can be accessed on the Statistics New Zealand Ariā site by entering the 121313 code on the front pages right-hand search function and then going to definitions.
