Supplementary Seasonal Employers can recruit temporary visa holders already in New Zealand to plant, maintain, harvest or pack crops when there are not enough New Zealanders available to do the work. You can apply for a Supplementary Season Employment work visa to work for a Supplementary Seasonal Employer.
