Find a Recognised Seasonal Employer

To apply for a Recognised Seasonal Employer Limited Visa you need a job offer from a New Zealand Recognised Seasonal Employer.

Recognised Seasonal Employers can recruit workers from overseas to work in our fruit and vegetable and grape-growing industries when they need extra workers.
