Occupational registration

Check if you need to be registered in your occupation to work in New Zealand

Find your occupation

In New Zealand you have to be registered to work in some jobs. You must get your registration before you can apply for a visa to work in one of these occupations.

If you do need to apply for occupational registration, contact the registration authority for your occupation.

Tip

Search the name of the registration you hold, not your job title.
