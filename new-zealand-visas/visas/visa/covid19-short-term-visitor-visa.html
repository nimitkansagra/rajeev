COVID-19 Short-term Visitor Visa

Visa details

Apply for this visa if you are unable to leave New Zealand because of COVID-19.

Length of stay

2Â months

Cost
From
NZD $211
With this visa you can
  • Remain lawful while making arrangements to depart New Zealand.
  • Study in New Zealand for the 2 months.
Things to note
  • You must be in New Zealand to apply for this visa and be genuinely unable to depart New Zealand due to the impacts of COVID-19.
  • You cannot work on this visa.
  • You cannot travel out of New Zealand and return on this visa.
  • You must apply online for this visa.

When we decide if your intentions are genuine (bona fide), we consider all the information:

  • you provide to support your application
  • we have about your personal circumstances, and
  • you provided in any previous applications.

Evidence can include:

  • your home has closed its border to returnees
  • attempts to book travel out of New Zealand
  • a departure date from New Zealand, if your booked travel date is after your visa expiry, or
  • attempts to contact your High Commission, Embassy or Consulate.
