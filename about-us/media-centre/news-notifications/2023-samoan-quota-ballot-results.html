2023 Samoan Quota ballot results

These are the successful registrations for the 2023 Samoan Quota ballot.

13 October 2023
5 minute read

The 2023 Samoan Quota draw took place on 6 October 2023.

Sufficient registrations have been drawn to allow up to 1650 Samoan citizens to apply for residence.

As each registration can contain multiple people (i.e. a family of 4 on 1 registration), the list of successful registrations below has been drawn to invite a total of 1650 people to apply for residence under the Samoan Quota.

If your registration number is listed

We will be in touch with you soon with information on how to apply for the Samoan Quota Resident Visa.

If your registration number is not listed

If your registration number is not on this list your number was not drawn. This means you cannot apply for New Zealand residence under the 2023 Samoan Quota.

You can register again to enter the ballot in 2024 when the Samoan Quota ballot reopens.
