Auckland drop box address for hand delivery 

Instead of posting paper documents, you can hand-deliver them into our drop box between 07:30 and 17:00, Monday to Friday.

The drop box is not open on public holidays and there is no public counter service.

The drop box address is:

Immigration New Zealand
Ground floor
20 Amersham Way
Manukau
