BJ4.25 Currency of an Expression of Interest

  1. An Expression of Interest (EOI) is current for a period of six months from the date of initial submission to the Pool unless no Pool selection of Expressions of Interest has occurred within that six-month period. Where this is the case, the EOI is current until such time as a selection from the Pool has occurred.
  2. An EOI that is no longer current will be withdrawn from the Pool.
  3. Notwithstanding (a), the Branch Manager of the Business Migration Branch can, if necessary, in relation to managing the number of places available under the Migrant Investment Policy, extend the currency of an EOI beyond the six-month timeframe.
  4. An EOI will also be withdrawn from the Pool if it is rejected after selection because it does not meet prerequisites for entry to the Pool and as a result no Invitation to Apply is issued.

Effective 27/07/2009
