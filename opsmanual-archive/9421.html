C6.25 Determining the claim of a detained claimant (01/10/1999)

Because the claimant is being detained, the refugee status officer should determine the claim as speedily as possible, ideally within one or 2 working days:

  1. of receiving submissions or comments on the interview report; or
  2. (if there are no submissions or comments on the interview report) of the earlier of:
    1. being notified that there are no submissions or comments on the interview report; or
    2. the time limit for making submissions or comments on the interview report; or
  3. (if there is no interview report) of the interview itself.

Effective 01/10/1999
