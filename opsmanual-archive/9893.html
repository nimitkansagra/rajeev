Russia (04/02/2002)

A Diplom ob okonchanii vysshego uchebnego zavedeniya – Diploma of Specialist with professional/specialist title – requiring four or more years of university level study is assessed as occupying Category B Level 7 of the Qualifications table and will therefore be awarded 10 points.

A Kandidat Nauk and a Doktor Nauk from the following institutions are assessed as occupying Category A Level 8 of the Qualifications Table and will therefore be awarded 12 points, provided the holder of the qualification also obtained their undergraduate (Bachelors) degree from an institution included on the List of Recognised Qualifications. If the Kandidat Nauk or Doktor Nauk is not preceded by an undergraduate degree from an institution included on the List of Recognised Qualifications, it is assessed as occupying Category B Level 7 of the Qualifications Table and will therefore be awarded 10 points.
