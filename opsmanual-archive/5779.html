G3.10 Points awarded for qualifications (26/07/1999)

G3.10.1 Points awarded for base qualification

Principal applicants must be awarded 10 points for a New Zealand base qualification or overseas qualification(s) accepted as being comparable with a New Zealand base qualification.

G3.10.5 Points awarded for advanced qualification

Principal applicants must be awarded 11 points for a New Zealand advanced qualification or overseas qualification(s) accepted as being comparable with a New Zealand advanced qualification.

G3.10.10 Points awarded for Masters degree or higher

Principal applicants must be awarded 12 points for a New Zealand Masters or doctoral degree, or an overseas qualification accepted as being comparable with a New Zealand Masters or doctoral degree.

Effective 26/07/1999
