Germany (04/09/2000)

Qualifications from the Former German Democratic Republic

A Diplom requiring four or more years of study awarded by a university-level institution, a Diplom-Ingenieur and a Diplom awarded on the basis of the Staatsexamen for teachers and a Zeugnis uber den Hochschulabschluss (without a Diplom) or a Hochschulingenieur are assessed as occupying Category B Level 7 of the Qualifications Table and will therefore be awarded 10 points.

Qualifications from the Federal Republic of Germany

A Diplom/Magister Artium/Staatsexamen requiring at least 8 semesters of study from a university, technical university, Gesamthochschule or specialised Hochschule; a Gradiort (Grad.)/Diplom [FH] awarded following at least six semesters of study at a Fachhochscule or Gesamthochschule or a Staatsexamen/Staatsprufung awarded following at least six semesters of study at a Padagogische Hochschule or university; and a Doktor as a first qualification are assessed as occupying Category B Level 7 of the Qualifications Table and will therefore be awarded 10 points.
