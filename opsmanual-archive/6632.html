BF3.15 The amount of ESOL tuition to be pre-purchased by non-principal applicants (20/11/2002)
  1. Unless BF4 applies, the amount of ESOL tuition to be pre-purchased is determined by the applicant's average IELTS score across all four bands (as shown in their "Overall Band" score in the IELTS Test Report Form) according to the following table.

  1. The charge includes the applicant's ESOL tuition entitlement, as well as the NZIS and Skill New Zealand administration costs.
  2. If an applicant has not submitted IELTS results when requested, the maximum charge of NZ$6,650 applies.
