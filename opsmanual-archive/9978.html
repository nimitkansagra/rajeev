Aviation Qualifications (24/07/2006)

Airline Pilot Transport Licenses from the following listed countries are assessed as occupying Level 6 of the Register and will therefore qualify for 50 points.

  • Australia
  • Canada
  • Fiji
  • India
  • Israel
  • Malaysia
  • New Zealand
  • People’s Republic of China
  • Russia
  • Singapore
  • South Africa
  • South Korea
  • Sri Lanka
  • Ukraine
  • United Kingdom
  • United States America

Effective 24/07/2006
