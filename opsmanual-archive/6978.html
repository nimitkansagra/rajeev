SM3.20 Currency of an Expression of Interest (23/05/2004)

Immigration Act 1987 s13B (3A) (c)

  1. An Expression of Interest is current for a period of three months from the date it is submitted to the Pool unless no Pool selection of Expressions of Interest has occurred within that three-month period. Where this is the case, the Expression of Interest is current until such time as a selection from the Pool of Expressions of Interest has occurred.
  2. An Expression of Interest that is no longer current will be withdrawn from the Pool.
  3. An Expression of Interest will also be withdrawn from the pool if it is rejected after selection because it does not meet prerequisites for entry to the Pool and as a result no invitation to apply has been issued.

SM3.20.1 Interim provisions for Expressions of Interest submitted to the Pool on or before 3 June 2004

Despite SM3.20 above, the following rules regarding the currency of Expressions of Interest are applicable for Expressions of Interest submitted to the Pool on or before 3 June 2004.

  1. Expressions of Interest submitted to the Pool from and including 17 December 2003 to and including 3 March 2004 are current until 3 September 2004.
  2. Expressions of Interest submitted to the Pool on and including 4 March 2004 to and including 3 June 2004 are current for a period of six months from the date they were submitted to the Pool.

Effective 23/05/2004
