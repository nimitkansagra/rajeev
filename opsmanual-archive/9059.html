Y2.30 When carrier is exempt from its responsibilities (01/10/1999)

Immigration Regulations 1999 reg 27(2)

The carrier and the person in charge of any craft carrying a person who is exempt from having to produce a passport or certificate of identity under regulation 27(1) are exempt from having to ensure under section 125(2)(a) of the Immigration Act 1987 that such a person holds a passport or certificate of identity.

Effective 01/10/1999
