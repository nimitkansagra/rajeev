F5.10 Verification of family details (26/07/1999)

Visa and immigration officers may refer to former applications lodged by applicants, family members of applicants, or sponsors in order to verify declarations made by applicants about their family details (such as the number of family members, the whereabouts of family members, or an applicant's marital status).

Effective 26/07/1999
