South Africa exceptions (04/02/2008)

National N3 Trade Certificates

National N3 Trade Certificates gained in South Africa together with evidence of a completed Contract of Apprenticeship registered with a recognised industry body, and evidence of the applicant passing their associated trade test, are assessed as occupying Level 3 of the Register.

Effective 04/02/2008
