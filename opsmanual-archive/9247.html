L2.15 Currency of limited purpose visas

Immigration Act 1987 s 14A

  1. All limited purpose visas are:
    1. current for travel until the date specified; and
    2. effective for one entry only (single).
  2. A limited purpose visa must not be issued unless the applicant's travel document is current for:
    1. at least 3 months beyond the expiry date of the proposed permit; or
    2. (only if the express purpose is urgent) one month, if the issuing Government has consular representation in New Zealand that is able to issue and renew travel documents.

Effective 01/10/1999
