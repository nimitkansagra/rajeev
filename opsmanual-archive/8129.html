P4.1 Health (28/11/2005)
  1. Participants in exchange schemes must have an acceptable standard of health (see E6.1).
  2. If the exchange period is to be longer than 12 months, before a visa is issued, inbound participants will be required to submit a fully completed Medical and Chest X-Ray Certificate NZIS 1007.

Effective 28/11/2005
