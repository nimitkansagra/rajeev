RW3.10 Evidential requirements

Applications for residence under the Residence policy for holders of work permits granted under the Talent (Arts, Culture and Sports)Work Policy must include:

  1. Evidence that during the currency of their visa and permit issued and granted under the Talent (Arts, Culture and Sports) Work Policy, the applicant has been active in their declared field of art, culture or sport throughout a period of 24 months in New Zealand; and
  2. Evidence that the applicant is still prominent in that field of art, culture or sport; and
  3. A completed Talent (Arts, Culture and Sports) Sponsorship Form from an organisation of national repute that includes the undertakings set out in RW3.5(b)(iii) above.

Effective 29/04/2002
