V3.115 Work permit holders dismissed during a trial period

  1. Holders of current work permits who:
    1. hold a permit subject to the condition that they may only take up employment with a specific employer; and
    2. had provision for a trial period of up to 90 days under Section 67A of the Employment Relations Act 2000 included in their employment agreement; and
    3. have been notified within the duration of the trial period that they are to be dismissed; and
    4. have not previously been the principal applicant in a successful application under this policy;

    may be granted a visitor’s permit for up to three months.

  2. Applicants for a visitor's permit under this policy must:
    1. meet funds or sponsorship requirements for visitors (see V2.20);
    2. provide evidence of travel tickets, onward travel arrangements or sufficient funds for the purchase of onward travel tickets (see V2.25);
    3. meet health and character requirements for temporary entry (see E6.1 and E6.5).
  3. Partners and dependent children may also be included in the principal applicant’s visitor’s permit application.

Effective 27/07/2009
