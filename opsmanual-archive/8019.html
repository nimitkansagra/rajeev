E2.35 Ban on the issue of work visas to Fijian citizens for the purpose of taking up work of a temporary seasonal nature

Despite the provisions of work policy, applications made for work visas for the purposes of work of a temporary seasonal nature by citizens of Fiji will be declined.

Effective 07/12/2006
