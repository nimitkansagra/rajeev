RW1 Objective

The objective of the Residence from Work category is to enable the grant of residence to people whose talents are needed by New Zealand employers, people with exceptional talent in a field of art, culture or sport and people working in areas of identified absolute occupational shortage in New Zealand.

Effective 29/04/2002
