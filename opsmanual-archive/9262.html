L3.40 Limitations to which holders of limited purpose permits are subject

Immigration Act 1987 ss 34D, 34F

The holder of a limited purpose permit is subject to the following limitations:

  1. the holder must leave New Zealand no later than the date on which the permit expires; and
  2. the holder may not apply for a permit of a different type, whether before or after the permit expires, while in New Zealand; and
  3. the holder may not request a special direction or a permit under section 35A of the Immigration Act 1987, whether before or after the permit expires, while in New Zealand; and
  4. the holder may not, whether before or after the permit expires, appeal to:
    1. the Removal Review Authority; or
    2. the Residence Appeal Authority; or
    3. the Deportation Review Tribunal; or
    4. the High Court; and
  5. the holder is immediately liable to removal if unlawfully in New Zealand after the limited purpose permit expires.

Effective 01/10/1999
