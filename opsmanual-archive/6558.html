BE2.1 Employee of a relocating business (20/11/2002)

Principal applicants in the Employees of Relocating Businesses category are required to demonstrate that:

  1. they are an employee of a relocating business and that they are a key employee; and
  2. the relocation of the business is supported by Trade NZ (New Zealand Trade Development Board), Industry New Zealand or another similar organisation.

Effective 20/11/2002
