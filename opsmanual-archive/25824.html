U7.30 Temporary exemption from the requirement to live with a legal guardian in New Zealand

  1. Students enrolled in school years 1 to 8, and students aged 13 and under who are enrolled in any other provider (see U3.30.10), may apply for a variation of conditions to their student permit to temporarily exempt them from the requirement to live with their legal guardian in New Zealand as set out at U3.30(b).
  2. A variation of conditions will only be granted where an immigration officer is satisfied that an emergency exists that requires the legal guardian to temporarily return to their home country.
  3. The applicant must provide evidence that:
    1. an emergency exists that requires the legal guardian to temporarily return to their home country; and
    2. the education provider has approved the alternative caregiving arrangements, consistent with its obligations under the Code of Practice for the Pastoral Care of International Students; and
    3. the legal guardian has purchased a return travel ticket.
  4. The variation of conditions may be granted for the period of time required, up to a maximum of four weeks.
  5. Any failure of the legal guardian to return to New Zealand within the approved period may result in the revocation of the student’s permit (see U7.15).

Effective 30/11/2009
