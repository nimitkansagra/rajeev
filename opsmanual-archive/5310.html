WF2 Partners of New Zealand citizens or residents (29/09/2003)

Partners (see E4.1.10) of New Zealand citizens or residents may be issued with a work visa and/or granted a work permit provided that:

  1. they are in a genuine and stable relationship (see E4.5.1); and
  2. the New Zealand partner intends to be in New Zealand for the same period of time applied for by the applicant; and
  3. the New Zealand partner supports the application.

Effective 29/09/2003
