prescribed manner

From policy: E4.40 How an application must be lodged (Generic temporary entry policy)

Immigration Regulations 1999 reg 3, 3A, 6A

  1. Applications must be lodged in the prescribed manner.
  2. The prescribed manner is the manner that meets the mandatory requirements laid down for applications of the relevant type in the Immigration Regulations 1999.
  3. Applications that are not lodged in the prescribed manner must not be accepted for processing (see E4.75).

Effective 01/07/2002
