R5.5 Evidential requirements

Immigration Regulations 1999 regs 4(2), 10(2)

  1. Visa and immigration officers must be satisfied that the information an applicant submits complies with the evidential requirements set out in Government residence policy.
  2. Even if an applicant meets the specific evidential requirements, a visa or immigration officer may still decide that additional evidence is necessary.
  3. Visa and immigration officers should use their discretion to decide what is sufficient evidence for policy criteria that have no specific evidential requirements.

Effective 26/07/1999
