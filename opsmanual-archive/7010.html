SM4.25 Migrant levy (17/12/2003)

Principal applicants approved-in-principle under the Skilled Migrant Category must pay a Migrant Levy (see R5.90) when:

  1. their application is approved-in-principle, unless:
  2. the Levy has been paid prior to the principal applicant being issued with a work visa or work permit for the purpose of becoming established in skilled employment in New Zealand.

Effective 17/12/2003
