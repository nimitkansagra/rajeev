G4.30 Confirmation of offer of employment

After an application has been approved in principle, but before a visa will be issued or a permit granted, visa and immigration officers must:

  1. be satisfied that the job offer for which points have been awarded is still current; and
  2. be provided with the original or a certified copy of the employment contract, if this has not been provided at the time of assessment.

Effective 26/07/1999
