WS5 Conditions of specific purpose or event work visas or permits (29/04/2002)
  1. Work visas issued and permits granted to applicants approved under this policy will be endorsed with conditions appropriate to the needs of the applicant in undertaking their specific purpose or event.

    Example: A work visa for a Business Investor category residence applicant should be endorsed as follows:

    i As Facilitate Direct Investment

    ii For Self

    iii At For any area

Effective 29/04/2002
