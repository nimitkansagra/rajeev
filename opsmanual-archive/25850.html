BL4 English language requirements

  1. Principal applicants in the Entrepreneur Plus Category must have a minimum overall band score of IELTS 4 for English language ability (see BF2).
  2. Any partner or dependent children aged 16 years and over who are included in the application must meet the minimum standard of English above or pre-purchase ESOL tuition (see BF1.1).

Effective 30/11/2009
