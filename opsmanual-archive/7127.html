WR3.10 Inclusion of occupations on the Priority Occupations List (29/04/2002)
  1. The Priority Occupations List is a list of occupations in which the NZIS, in consultation with Industry New Zealand, relevant industry groups and unions, has identified an absolute (sustained and ongoing) shortage of skilled workers.
  2. The Priority Occupations List is updated six-monthly.

WR3.10.1 The Priority Occupations List

The Priority Occupations List is held at Appendix 6 of the NZIS Operational Manual.

Effective 29/04/2002
