E4.50 Requirements for lodging an application for a temporary permit at a customs place

Immigration Act 1987 ss 25(1), 126
Immigration Regulations 1999 reg 11

  1. An application for a temporary permit at a customs place must be made on an approved arrival card.
  2. The application must be submitted to an immigration officer, together with the applicant's current passport or certificate of identity and, if appropriate, the applicant's temporary visa.
  3. Before determining the application, the immigration officer may require the applicant to produce:
    1. travel tickets or evidence of onward travel arrangements, and
    2. evidence of funds for maintenance or of sponsorship.

Effective 26/07/1999
