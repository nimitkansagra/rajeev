W6.10 Japan-New Zealand Working Holiday Scheme (01/07/2001)
  1. This scheme allows young Japanese citizens to undertake casual employment in New Zealand to supplement their funds during their holiday by issuing them with work visas authorising a permit for a maximum of 12 months.
  2. To be eligible for a work visa under this scheme, an applicant must:
    1. be resident in Japan at the time of applying, and
    2. provide evidence of onward travel and maintenance and accommodation, and
    3. have a minimum of NZ$4200 available funds for maintenance during their first 6 months in New Zealand.
  3. A successful applicant may be issued with a single or multiple journey visa, endorsed as follows:
    • 'New Zealand/Japan Working Holiday Scheme' and 'Funds Varied'
    • As: 'employee'
    • For: 'for any employer'
    • At: 'for any area'.
  4. The work permit is granted on arrival in New Zealand on condition that the holder:
    1. does not work for the same employer for more than 3 months, and
    2. does not undertake permanent or long-term employment unless they apply for and obtain a permit that enables this as provided by W6.5.1, and
    3. is not eligible to come to New Zealand for a further working holiday under this scheme, and
    4. may undertake English language studies for up to 3 months at a private college or technical institute, provided that they attend the course on a full-fee basis.

Effective 01/07/2001
