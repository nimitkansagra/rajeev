BL2 Summary of requirements

  1. For an application under the Entrepreneur Plus Category to be approved:
    1. the principal applicant and family members included in the application must meet health and character requirements (see A4 and A5); and
    2. the principal applicant must hold a Long Term Business Permit; and
    3. the principal applicant and family members over 16 years must meet minimum English language requirements (see BL4); and
    4. the principal applicant must transfer NZ$0.5 million to New Zealand (see BL5).
    5. the principal applicant must have successfully established a business in New Zealand (see BL6).

Effective 30/11/2009
