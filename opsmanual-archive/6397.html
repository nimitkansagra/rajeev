BA3 Streamlining

  1. Applications under the Business Immigration Policy will be given priority processing.
  2. Applications under the Business Immigration Policy are to be determined only by visa or immigration officers known as business immigration specialists.
  3. Despite (b) above, applications under the Investor category may be determined by visa and immigration officers other than business immigration specialists where this is directed by the Chief Operating Officer of the New Zealand Immigration Service.
  4. NZIS Branch Managers will provide liaison services for the business immigration specialists to facilitate contact with applicants and the processing of business immigration applications.

Effective 20/11/2002
