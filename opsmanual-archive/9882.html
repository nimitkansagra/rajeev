Romania (15/12/2003)

A Diploma de Licenta or a Diploma de Stat, such as a Diploma de Inginer, requiring at least four years of full-time study from the following institutions are assessed as occupying Level 7 of the Register and will therefore qualify for 50 points.

A Diploma de Doctor from the following institutions is assessed as occupying Level 9 or 10 of the Register and will therefore qualify for 55 points.

  • Academia de Muzica din Bucuresti (The Bucharest Academy of Music)
  • Academia de Muzica din Cluj (Academy Of Music, Cluj-Napoca)
  • Acedemia de Studii Economice, Bucuresti (Academy of Economics, Bucharest)
  • Academia de Teatru si Film, Bucuresti (Academy of Theatre and Film)
  • Conservatorul de Muzica din Iasi (Conservatory of Music, Iasi [Jassy])
  • Institutul Agronomic din Bucuresti (Institute of Agriculture, Bucharest)
  • Institutal Agronomic din Cluj-Napoca (Institute of Agriculture, Cluj-Napoca)
  • Institutal Agronomic din Iasi (Institute Of Agriculture, Iasi)
  • Institutal Agronomic din Timisoara (Institute of Agriculture, Timisoara)
  • Institutul de Arhitectura din Bucuresti (Institute of Architecture, Bucharest)
  • Institutul de Arte Plastice din Bucuresti (Institute of Plastic Arts, Academia de Bucharest)
  • Institutul de Arte Plastice din Cluj-Napoca (Institute of Plastic Arts, Cluj-Napoca)
  • Institutul de Constructii din Bucuresti (Building Institute, Bucharest)
  • Institutul de Medicina si Farmacie, Bucuresti (institute of Medicine and Pharmacy, Bucharest)
  • Institutul de Medicina si Farmacie, Cluj-Napoca (Institute of Medicine and Pharmacy, Cluj-Napoca)
  • Institutul de Medicina si Farmacie, Iasi (Institute of Medicine and Pharmacy, Iasi [Jassy])
  • Institutul de Medicina Timisoara (Institute of Medicine, Timisoara)
  • Institutul National de Chimie (National Institute of Chemistry)
  • Institutul de Petrol si Gaze, Ploesti (Institute of Petroleum and Gas, Ploesti)
  • Institutul Politehnic din Bucuresti (Technical Institute of Bucharest)
  • Institutul Politehnic din Cluj-Napoca (Technical Institute of Cluj-Napoca)
  • Institutul Politehnic din Iasi (Technical Institute of Iasi [Jassy])
  • Institutul Politehnic din Timisoara (Technical Institute of Timisoara)
  • Institutul de Teatru din Tirgu-Mures (Institute of Dramatic Art, Tirgu Mures)
  • Universitatea din Brasov (University of Brasov)
  • Universitatea din Bucuresti (University Of Bucharest)
  • Universitatea din Cluj-Napoca (University of Cluj-Napoca)
  • Universitatea din Craiova (University of Craiova)
  • Universitatea din Galati (University of Galati)
  • Universitatea din Iasi (University of Iasi [Jassy])
  • Universitatea Tehnica, Oradea (Technical University, Oradea)
  • Universitatea din Timisoara (University of Timosoara)
