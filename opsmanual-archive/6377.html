BD Entrepreneur Category (20/11/2002)

This category applies to persons whose applications are made under this category prior to 20 November 2002 and those people who hold a work visa and or a work permit issued and/or granted under the Long Term Business category prior to 20 November 2002.

Effective 20/11/2002
