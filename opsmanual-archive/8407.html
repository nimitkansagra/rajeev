V3.10 Spouses or de facto partners and dependent children of student or work visa or permit holders

Subject to the provisions of E4.5:

  1. Spouses or de facto partners (see E4.1.10) of student or work visa or permit holders may be issued with visitor visas or granted visitor permits if that type of visa or permit is appropriate to their needs for the currency of the spouse or partner's visa or permit.
  2. Dependent children (see E4.1.5) of student or work visa or permit holders may be issued with visitor visas or granted visitor permits if that type of visa or permit is appropriate to their needs for the currency of the parent's visa or permit.
  3. Notwithstanding (a) and (b) above, spouses, partners and dependent children of persons who were unlawfully in New Zealand immediately before being granted a temporary permit under the October 2000 Transitional Policy are not eligible for the issue of a visa or grant of a permit under this policy.

Effective 01/04/2001
