BC1 Objective

This category caters for business people who are interested in establishing a business in New Zealand:

  1. without living permanently in New Zealand, or
  2. with the intention of better enabling themselves to meet the relevant criteria for residence under the Entrepreneur category.

Effective 20/11/2002
