BA2.1 Residence categories (20/11/2002)

The residence categories of the Business Immigration Policy are part of the Government residence policy and are:

  • Investor category (see BB)
  • Entrepreneur category (see BD) (for people whose applications are made prior to 20 November 2002 and people who hold work visas and/or work permits issued and granted under the Long Term Business Visa/Permit category prior to 20 November 2002)
  • Entrepreneur category (see BH)
  • Employees of relocating businesses category (see BE)

BA2.1.1 Generic policy provisions

The residence categories have generic policy provisions covering the following matters:

  • English language requirements (see BF)
  • Payment of settlement information fee (see R5.85)
  • Payment of migrant levy (see R5.90)
  • Health and character requirements (see R4)

Effective 20/11/2002
