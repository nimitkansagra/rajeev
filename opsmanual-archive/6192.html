R5.35 Later application under Family category by previous principal applicant (26/07/1999)

An applicant for residence in New Zealand under the Family category policy must not be approved for residence under that policy if their application is based on their relationship to a New Zealand resident or citizen who originally obtained residence as the spouse, de facto partner or dependent child(ren) of that applicant.

Effective 26/07/1999
