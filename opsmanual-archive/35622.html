Sri Lanka exceptions

The following qualifications gained in Sri Lanka are assessed as occupying Level 3 of the NZQF.

  • Certificate of Proficiency as Skilled Craftsman - Mason
  • Certificate of Proficiency as Skilled Craftsman - Electronics
  • Certificate of Proficiency as Skilled Craftsman - Electrician
  • Certificate of Proficiency as Skilled Craftsman - Machinist
  • Certificate of Proficiency as Skilled Craftsman - Automobile Mechanic
  • Certificate of Proficiency as Skilled Craftsman - Auto Electrician
  • Certificate of Proficiency as Skilled Craftsman - Machinist
  • Certificate of Proficiency as Skilled Craftsman - Motor Boat Mechanic
  • National Trade Test Grade 1 - Electrical Wireman
  • National Trade Test Grade 1 - Carpentry
  • National Trade Test Grade 1 - Masonry
  • National Trade Test Grade 1 - Plumber
  • National Trade Test Grade 1 - Masonry (Block Mason)
  • National Trade Test Grade 1 - Steel Fixing and Bar Bending
  • National Trade Test Grade 1 - Vehicle Plant Mechanic
  • National Trade Test Grade 1 - Motor Scraper Operator
  • National Trade Test Grade 1 - Hydraulic Type Crane Operator
  • National Trade Test Grade 1 - Hydraulic Excavator Operator
  • National Trade Test Grade 1 - Automotive Mechanic
  • National Trade Test Grade 1 - Fitter (General)
  • National Trade Test Grade 1 - Machinist (General)
  • National Trade Test Grade 1 - Welder
  • National Trade Test Grade 1 - Electrician (Power)
  • National Trade Test Grade 1 - Refrigeration and Air Conditioning Mechanic
  • National Trade Test Grade 1 - Precast Concrete Layer
  • National Trade Test Grade 1 - Roofer (Wooden)
  • National Trade Test Grade 1 - Roadman
  • National Trade Test Grade 1 - Construction Supervisor
  • National Trade Test Grade 1 - Plasterer
  • National Trade Test Grade 1 - Cabinet Maker
  • National Trade Test Grade 1 - Cupboard Maker
  • National Trade Test Grade 1 - T.V., Radio and Electronics Mechanic
  • National Trade Test Grade 1 - Automobile Electrician
