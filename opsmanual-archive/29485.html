WL2.10 Determining an application for a Silver Fern Job Search visa

In order to be issued with a Silver Fern Job Search visa, applicants must:

  1. have been aged between 20 and 35 years inclusive at the time the application was lodged; and
  2. be outside of New Zealand; and
  3. hold a qualification that meets the Silver Fern Job Search Policy qualification requirements at WL2.15 and the requirements for recognition under Skilled Migrant Policy set out at SM14.10; and
  4. meet the English language requirements set out at SM5.5; and
  5. have a minimum of NZ$4,200 available funds for their maintenance during the period of stay in New Zealand; and
  6. be genuine in their intention to gain skilled employment; and
  7. meet temporary entry health and character policy requirements (E6.1 and E6.5); and
  8. have not previously been issued a Silver Fern Job Search visa.

Effective 29/03/2010
