G3.10 New Zealand qualifications (04/09/2000)
  1. Principal applicants must be awarded a maximum of two additional points for a New Zealand qualification if:
    1. they have been awarded points for the qualification, or
    2. they were eligible to be awarded points for the qualification but did not claim them.
  2. Qualifications gained with NZODA funding will not be awarded the additional points, and principal applicants wishing to gain the additional points must submit evidence when their application is made that they did not receive NZODA funding. Evidence is a letter from the Scholarship Management Unit of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Trade (MFAT)'s Development Co-operation Division, indicating that the principal applicant did not receive NZODA funding to complete the qualification.

Effective 04/09/2000
