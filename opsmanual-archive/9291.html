L5.40 How a direct application must be lodged

Immigration Regulations 1999 regs 3, 7, 14, 15

  1. Applications must be lodged in the prescribed manner.
  2. The prescribed manner is the manner that meets the mandatory requirements laid down for applications for limited purpose visas or permits in regulations 7, 14 and 15 of the Immigration Regulations 1999 depending on the nature of the express purpose.
  3. Applications that are not lodged in the prescribed manner must not be accepted for processing (see L5.65).

Effective 01/10/1999
