NOTES:

++ USA - Visitor's, student and work visa applications from applicants residing in Alaska, Arizona, California, Colorado, Hawaii, Idaho, Montana, Nevada, New Mexico, Oregon, Utah, Wyoming and Washington State should be forwarded to Los Angeles. Visitor's, student and work visa applications from applicants residing in other states and all residence and returning residents' visas applications should be forwarded to Washington DC.

** Transit Visas are required for citizens of these countries intending to transit New Zealand unless their intermediate or final destination after transiting New Zealand is Australia and they hold a current visa issued by the Government of Australia to enter Australia; or hold another current visa to enter New Zealand.

# London is the designated receiving office for residence applications. Residence applications may be accepted at either London or by Pretoria on behalf of London. The fee payable is the fee that applies to residence applications lodged in London.

^ Washington is the designated receiving office for Family Category residence applications only. All Skilled Migrant Category and Business applications from the Americas must go to London. The fee payable is the fee that applies to residence and business applications lodged in London.

Applicants for working holiday visas from Canada, the Netherlands, Malaysia and Singapore can lodge their applications at any INZ branch (onshore or offshore), or the NZ Embassy in Washington DC or Tokyo.

» This is the designated receiving office for fee purposes. The fee payable for the application is determined by this receiving office.

§ Applications should be lodged with this receiving office.

Applications from Palestinian passport holders should be forwarded to the appropriate receiving office for their country of residence. For example, Palestinian passport holders living in Lebanon should forward visitor's student and work visa applications to Dubai, and residence applications to Bangkok. If living in Israel all applications should be forwarded to Bangkok.

Applications for residence under the Residence to Work policies must be lodged in New Zealand.
