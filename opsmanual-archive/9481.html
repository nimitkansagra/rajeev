Notes:

* Samoan citizens and persons applying under the residence policy for Victims of Domestic Violence are entitled to a $90 fee reduction on applications for residence. No one to whom this applies is entitled to more than a $90 fee reduction on any single application.

x Any residence applicant who, on or after 1 July 2003, has paid the fee specified in the table above for a long term business visa or permit, Talent (accredited employers), Talent (arts, culture, & sports), or Priority Occupations List application is entitled to a $90 reduction of their residence application fee. No one to who this applies is entitled to more than a $90 fee reduction on any single application.

** Citizens of Samoa are entitled to a $90 fee reduction on all applications for long term business visas and permits and work visas and permits under the talent and priority occupations list work policies. No one to whom this applies is entitled to more than a $90 fee reduction on any single application.

*** Citizens of Cook Islands, Solomon Islands, Fiji, Kiribati, Tahiti, Marshall Islands, Tokelau, Tuvalu, New Caledonia, Tonga, Nauru, Niue, Vanuatu, and Samoa transiting through New Zealand on their way to or from the Pacific Islands are exempted from the transit visa fee.
