BC2.1 Special category of work visa/permit (26/07/1999)

Long-term business visas/permits are a special category of work visa/permit. As well as meeting the specific policy requirements of this special category applicants must also meet the requirements under Generic Temporary Entry Policy.

Effective 26/07/1999
