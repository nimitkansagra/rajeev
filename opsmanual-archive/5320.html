SM5.15 English language requirements for partners where bonus points are claimed for the partner's skilled employment or recognised qualifications

A partner's skilled employment in New Zealand (see SM10) or recognised qualifications (see SM17), only qualify for points if the partner meets the English language requirements for principal applicants (see SM5.5 above).
