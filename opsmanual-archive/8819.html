WI4.85 Uruguay Working Holiday Scheme (30/07/2007)
  1. This scheme allows 100 young citizens of the Oriental Republic of Uruguay annually to be issued a work visa, valid for travel for 12 months from date of issue, and upon arrival in New Zealand to be granted a work permit for a maximum of 12 months.
  2. Successful applicants will also be issued a multiple entry work visa, valid for the same period as the work permit, allowing re-entry to New Zealand on any number of occasions.
  3. To be eligible for a work visa or permit under this scheme, applicants must:
    1. be a citizen of Uruguay, ordinarily resident in the Oriental Republic of Uruguay at the time of applying, and
    2. have a minimum of NZ$4,200 available funds for their maintenance during the period of stay in New Zealand, and
    3. agree to hold medical and comprehensive hospitalisation insurance that will remain current throughout their stay in New Zealand.
  4. Participants in this scheme must not work for the same employer for a period exceeding three months.
  5. Applicants under this scheme must lodge their application in the prescribed manner (E4.45).

Effective 30/07/2007
