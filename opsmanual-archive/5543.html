A15.4 Instruments of Delegation 2005 (25/05/2005)

Delegation of Powers to Department of Labour Workforce - Immigration Services

I, PAUL DESMOND SWAIN, MINISTER OF IMMIGRATION, acting pursuant to section 131(1) of the Immigration Act 1987, hereby:

  1. delegate to every immigration officer who is the holder (or is acting as the holder) of any of the offices or positions listed below, or whose name and/or position is listed in Appendix 1 to this instrument:
    1. the powers delegated under Schedule 1 in the Instrument of Delegation signed by the Minister of Immigration on 30 September 1999; and
    2. the powers delegated in the Instrument of Delegation signed by the Minister of Immigration on 19 February 2004; and
    3. the powers delegated in the Instrument of Delegation signed by the Minister of Immigration on 8 April 2004;
  2. delegate to every immigration officer whose name and/or position is listed in Appendix 2 to this instrument:
    1. the powers delegated under Schedule 2 in the Instrument of Delegation signed by the Minister of Immigration on 30 September 1999; and
    2. the powers delegated in the Instrument of Delegation signed by the Minister of Immigration on 19 February 2004; and
    3. the powers delegated in the Instrument of Delegation signed by the Minister of Immigration on 8 April 2004;
  3. delegate to every immigration officer whose name and/or position is listed in Appendix 3 to this instrument, the powers delegated under Schedule 3 in the Instrument of Delegation signed by the Minister of Immigration on 30 September 1999;
  4. delegate to every immigration officer whose name and/or position is listed in Appendix 4 to this instrument, the powers delegated under Schedule 4 in the Instrument of Delegation signed by the Minister of Immigration on 30 September 1999;
  5. delegate to every immigration officer who is an immigration officer by virtue of being a customs officer designated by the Secretary of Labour as an immigration officer, whether individually or by class or position, the powers delegated under Schedule 5 in the Instrument of Delegation signed by the Minister of Immigration on 30 September 1999.

Dated at Wellington this 25th day of May, 2005.

Hon Paul Desmond SwainMinister of Immigration
