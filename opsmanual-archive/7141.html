Long Term Skill Shortage List (04/07/2005)

This Appendix is part of Government immigration policy as described in Section 13A of the Immigration Act 1987 (see WR3.10.1) and Government residence policy as described in section 13B of the Immigration Act 1987 (see RW4).

Occupational Groups

Occupation

Long Term Skill Shortage List Requirements

Qualifications must be comparable to the standard of the New Zealand qualification listed

In order to claim bonus points for qualifications in an area of absolute shortage under the Skilled Migrant Category OR for the purposes of the Job Search Policy for General Skills Category, applicants must have the following qualifications.

Qualifications must be comparable to the standard of the New Zealand qualification listed. Also see Note at the end of this list
