U8.20 Dependants of holders of work permits (01/11/1999)
  1. Spouses and partners and dependent children of work permit holders are regarded as domestic students for all fee purposes at primary and secondary schools for the period of the work permit.
  2. Spouses and partners and dependent children of work permit holders who study at tertiary level are regarded as fee-paying students and must pay foreign student fees.
  3. Dependants of work visa/permit holders may be issued with student visas or granted student permits to enable attendance at primary or secondary school without the need to produce evidence of enrollment.
  4. Guarantees of accommodation and/or maintenance for dependants may be waived provided this is covered by the income of the spouse, partner or parent or by evidence of funds, sponsorship or guarantees submitted with the work visa/permit application of the spouse, partner or parent (see W2.25).

Effective 01/11/1999
