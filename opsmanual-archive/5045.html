U7.15 Revocation of a student permit (01/07/2004)

Immigration Act 1987 ss 27(3), 33
Immigration Regulations 1999 reg 22

  1. A student permit holder who breaches any of the conditions of their permit (in particular those relating to attendance, employment, and living with their guardian) is liable:
    1. to have the permit revoked, and
    2. to be required to leave or be removed from New Zealand.
  2. Where the student is required to live with a guardian (see U3.30), and the permit of that guardian is revoked, the permit of the student will also be revoked.

Effective 01/07/2004
