Appendix 4 - Receiving Offices (04/12/2006)

IMPORTANT: This is a list of receiving offices. The processing of applications may occur in other offices. Information on the address, phone and fax numbers of receiving offices and the fees payable for applications can be found on NZIS website at http://www.immigration.govt.nz under "Offices and Fees".

Skilled Migrant Category:

The list of Skilled Migrant receiving offices for offshore applications is as follows:

Bangkok - for applicants residing in Cambodia, Indonesia, Israel, Jordan, Lao, Myanmar, Oman, Philippines, Saudi Arabia, Thailand, United Arab Emirates, Vietnam, and all other Middle Eastern countries.

Beijing - for applicants residing in China, Hong Kong, Japan, and South Korea.

London - for applicants residing in Africa, Europe, UK, USA, and North Marian Islands.

New Delhi - for applicants residing in India and Sri Lanka.

Singapore - for applicants residing in Malaysia or Singapore.

NOTES:

++ USA:
Visitor's, student and work visa applications from applicants residing in Alaska, Arizona, California, Colorado, Hawaii, Idaho, Montana, Nevada, New Mexico, Oregon, Utah, Wyoming and Washington State should be forwarded to LOS ANGELES. Visitor's, student and work visa applications from applicants residing in other states and all residence and returning residents' visas applications should be forwarded to WASHINGTON DC.

** Transit Visas required for citizens of these countries intending to transit New Zealand either on the way to, or coming from Cook Islands, Fiji, Kiribati, Marshall Islands, Nauru, New Caledonia, Niue, Samoa, Solomon Islands, Tahiti, Tokelau, Tonga, Tuvalu, or Vanuatu, unless their intermediate or final destination after transiting New Zealand is Australia and they hold a current visa issued by the Government of Australia to enter Australia; or hold another current visa to enter New Zealand.

*** A transit visa is required for all Nepalese passport holders intending to transit New Zealand en route to any destination unless the person's intermediate or final destination after transiting New Zealand is Australia and they hold a current visa to enter Australia, or they are a holder of another current visa to enter New Zealand.

# London is the designated receiving office for residence applications. Residence applications may be accepted at either London or by Pretoria on behalf of London. The fee payable is the fee that applies to residence applications lodged in London.

^ Washington is the designated receiving office for Family Category residence applications only. All Skilled migrant and Business applications from the Americas must go to London. The fee payable is the fee that applies to residence and business applications lodged in London.

Applicants for working holiday visas from Canada, the Netherlands, Malaysia and Singapore can lodge their applications at any NZIS branch (onshore or offshore), or the NZ Embassy in Washington DC or Tokyo.

New Zealand

Citizens of any country who are in New Zealand may make application for any type of visa or permit at any visa and permit branch of the NZIS. Branches are located in Auckland, Manukau, Hamilton, Palmerston North, Wellington, Christchurch and Dunedin.
