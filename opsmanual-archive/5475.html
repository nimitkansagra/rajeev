U5.1 Status of education providers and courses (24/03/2003)
  1. All education providers must certify in their offers of places to foreign students (see U3.5) that the course offered complies with foreign student policy requirements as set out below for different kinds of institution.
  2. All education providers offering places to foreign students for courses of more than three months must be signatories to the Ministry of Education's Code of Practice for Pastoral Care of International Students.
  3. Any queries on the status of courses offered by primary and secondary schools (state, integrated or private) and private training establishments should be referred to the Approvals and Accreditations Service, New Zealand Qualifications Authority, P O Box 160, Wellington.

Effective 24/03/2003
