BF3.20 Failure to pre-purchase ESOL tuition (26/07/1999)

Any ESOL tuition charge due must be paid before a residence visa is issued or a residence permit is granted; and if it is not paid to the NZIS within the specified time, the residence application must be declined.

Effective 26/07/1999
