SM14.25 List of Recognised Qualifications (17/12/2003)

The List of Recognised Qualifications is a list of qualifications which may qualify for points without the need for a NZQA assessment (unless SM14.20 (d) applies). The List of Recognised Qualifications is contained in Appendix 5 of this Manual.

Effective 17/12/2003
