self-employment

From policy: BC2.1 Definition of self employment (LTBV policy)

  1. Self-employment is lawful active involvement in the management and operating of a business in New Zealand which the principal applicant has established or purchased, or in which the principal applicant has made a substantial investment (see (b) below).
  2. Substantial investment is defined as the purchase of 25% or more of the shareholding of a business.
  3. For the avoidance of doubt, self-employment does not include involvement of a passive or speculative nature.

Effective 20/11/2002

From policy BL6.5 Definitions (Entrepreneur Plus Category)

BL6.5.5 Self-employment

Self-employment is lawful active involvement in the management and operation of a business in New Zealand which the principal applicant has established or purchased, or in which the principal applicant has made a substantial investment (see BL6.5.1).
