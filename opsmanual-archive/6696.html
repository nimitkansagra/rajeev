BH2.10 English language requirements

Principal applicants in the Entrepreneur category must meet the minimum standard of English (see BF2).

Any partner or dependent children aged 16 years and over who are included in the application must meet a minimum standard of English or, where policy allows, pre-purchase of ESOL tuition (see BF1.1).

Effective 29/09/2003
