RW5.55 Refunds of ESOL tuition money

  1. If ESOL tuition money is paid but the principal applicant and partner and dependent children included in the application do not take up residence, a refund may be granted upon request to the NZIS. The request must be made in writing.
  2. Requests made more than 6 months after the expiry date of any unused residence visa must be declined.
  3. Visa and immigration officers considering requests for refunds must be satisfied that the principal applicant and partner and dependent children included in the application:
    1. have not taken up residence, and
    2. do not hold current residence visas or returning residents' visas.
  4. The person who paid the fee will be repaid only the ESOL entitlement. The NZIS and TEC administration costs will not be refunded.

Effective 29/09/2003
