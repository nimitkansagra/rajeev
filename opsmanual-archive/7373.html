G3.20 Verification of qualifications (04/09/2000)

G3.20 Verification of qualifications

Visa and immigration officers must be satisfied that documents provided as evidence of qualifications are genuine and accurate, and they may take any steps they determine necessary to verify such documents and the information they contain (see Z).

Effective 04/09/2000
