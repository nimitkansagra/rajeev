BD2.1 Successful establishment of a business in New Zealand that is benefiting the country

Principal applicants in the Entrepreneur category are required to demonstrate that:

  1. they have established a business in New Zealand; and
  2. the business is benefiting New Zealand in some way.

Effective 26/07/1999
