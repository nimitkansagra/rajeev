R6.1 New Zealand Residence Programme

  1. The New Zealand Residence Programme consists of all persons approved for residence in each year ending 30 June.
  2. The New Zealand Residence Programme is set on an annual basis, and any changes will be advised by an amendment to this policy.
  3. The New Zealand Residence Programme for the 2010/11 year is 45,000 to 50,000 approved places.

Effective 01/07/2010
