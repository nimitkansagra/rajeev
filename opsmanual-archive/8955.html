W3.155 Special work visas and permits for spouses and partners of holders of Long Term Business Visas and Permits

W3.155.1 Who is eligible for a special work visa or permit

  1. Spouses and de facto partners of people issued Long Term Business Visas or granted Long Term Business Permits may apply for a multiple entry work visa and work permit under this policy.
  2. Work visas and work permits will be issued or granted for the same period as the Long Term Business Visa and Long Term Business Permit held by the applicant’s spouse or de facto partner.

W3.155.5 Making an application

  1. Applications must be made in the prescribed manner (see E4.55); but an appropriately delegated immigration officer may waive:
    1. the application fee, and
    2. the requirement to produce evidence of funds or sponsorship, and
    3. the requirement to produce evidence of travel tickets or onward travel arrangements, and
    4. the requirement to produce a job offer.
  2. Any work permits granted under this provision may be endorsed with conditions that allow work for any employer.

Effective 04/12/2000
