BI10.15 Meeting the requirements (04/07/2005)

At the end of the five-year investment period, principal applicants must submit evidence to the Department of Labour that they have met the requirements imposed under section 18A of the Immigration Act 1987 (see BI12).

Effective 04/07/2005
