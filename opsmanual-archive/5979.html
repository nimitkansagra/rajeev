A15.10 Immigration Act 1987

Instrument of Designation

Pursuant to Section 2(1) of the Immigration Act, as amended by Section 2(4) of the Immigration Amendment Act 1999, I, Colin James McKenzie, Secretary of Labour, hereby:

Designate: Officers of Customs allocated to immigration and emigration duties at international airports and seaports, as immigration officers.

Dated at Wellington this 12th day of November 1991

C J McKenzie
Secretary of Labour

Effective 26/07/1999
