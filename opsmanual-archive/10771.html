substantial investment

From policy: BD3.5 Definitions (Entrepreneur Category) and BL6.5 Definitions (Entrepreneur Plus Category)

BL6.5 Substantial investment

Substantial investment means the purchase of 25% or more of the shareholding of a business.

Effective 30/11/2009

BD3.5.1 Substantial investment

Substantial investment means the purchase of 25% or more of the shareholding of a business.

Effective 26/07/1999
