G3.20 Evidential requirements for qualifications (1/3/2000)
  1. Evidence of a qualification is the original or certified copy of the qualification.
  2. If a qualification has been completed at a New Zealand institution, but the qualification document has not yet been conferred, a written statement from the issuing institution that the applicant has met the requirements to graduate may be acceptable as evidence of the qualification.
  3. Other documents that show subjects studied and marks obtained may also be required.

G3.20.1 Evidence of NZQA assessment of comparability

Acceptable evidence of NZQA's assessment of comparability is an original or certified copy of an NZQA assessment report.

G3.20.5 Evidence of comparability for the purposes of G3.1(b)(ii)

Evidence of comparability for the purposes of G3.1(b)(ii) is the original or certified copy of the certificate of full registration from the New Zealand registration organisation.

Effective 01/03/2000
