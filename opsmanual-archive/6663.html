BF2.10 Evidence of employment in New Zealand 26/07/1999)
  1. Evidence of full-time employment in New Zealand for a minimum of 12 months is original or certified copies of:
    1. references from employers on company letterhead, which state the occupation and dates of employment and the contact phone number and a address of the employer, or an employment contract with confirmation from the employer that the applicant is still employed.
  2. Evidence that English was the language of employment is a written statement from the employer that English was the primary language of employment.

Effective 26/07/1999
