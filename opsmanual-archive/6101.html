++ USA:
Visitor visa applicants residing in Alaska, Arizona, California, Colorado, Hawaii, Idaho, Montana, Nevada, New Mexico, Oregon, Utah, Wyoming, and Washington State should forward their applications to LOS ANGELES. All other visitor visa applicants and all applicants seeking
residence, returning resident's visas, student or work visas should forward their applications to WASHINGTON DC.

** Transit Visas required for citizens of these countries

# London is the designated receiving office for residence applications. Residence applications may be accepted at either London or by Pretoria on behalf of London. The fee payable is the fee that applies to residence applications lodged in London.

## Effective 1 October 2001

Applicants for working holiday visas from Canada, the Netherlands, Malaysia and Singapore can lodge their applications at any NZIS branch (onshore or offshore), or the NZ Embassy in Washington DC or Tokyo.
