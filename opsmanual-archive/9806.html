Indonesia (15/12/2003)

An SI (Stratum/Strata I) Sarjana, awarded by the following public institutions after 1990 and requiring four to seven years of full-time study (140-160 credits) for completion is assessed as occupying Level 7 of the Register and will therefore qualify for 50 points. Postgraduate degrees and diplomas require an NZQA assessment for points allocation.

Qualifications awarded between the introduction of the current system in 1979 and 1990 require assessment by NZQA.
