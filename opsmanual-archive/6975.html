SM3.17 Resubmission of Expressions of Interest to the Pool (Expressions of Interest selected on or before 21 December 2005)

  1. Expressions of Interest selected from the Pool on or before 21 December 2005 that:
    1. on 21 December 2005 have not resulted in an invitation to apply for residence; and
    2. do not include a claim for points for an offer of skilled employment in New Zealand or current skilled employment in New Zealand; and
    3. have a claim of total points of 100 or 105;

    will not be invited to apply for residence on the basis of that Expression of Interest, unless the person expressing interest in that Expression of Interest is in New Zealand on 21 December 2005.

  2. People affected by (a) above may submit a further Expression of Interest to the Pool up to and including 21 June 2006 and no fee will be payable for this Expression of Interest.

Effective 21/12/2005
