U6.36 Issue of student visas to applicants granted full student permits

  1. Any applicant granted a full student permit as provided by U6.35 should also be issued with a multiple student visa as specified in U6.30 at the same time the student permit is granted.
  2. No separate application form or additional fee is required.

Effective 24/03/2003
