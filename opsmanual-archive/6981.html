SM3.20 Currency of an Expression of Interest (17/12/2003)

Immigration Act 1987 s13B (3A) (c)

  1. An Expression of Interest will be withdrawn from the Pool if:
    1. it has remained in the pool for a period of three months; and
    2. it has not been selected from the Pool during that period; and
    3. a selection of Expressions of Interest from the Pool has occurred within that period.
  2. If no Pool selection of Expressions of Interest has occurred within the three-month period that an Expression of Interest has been in the Pool, that Expression of Interest will remain in the Pool until such time as a selection from the Pool of Expressions of Interest has occurred.
  3. An Expression of Interest will also no longer be current if it is rejected after selection because it does not meet prerequisites for entry to the Pool and as a result no invitation to apply has been issued.

Effective 17/12/2003
