Iran (04/02/2002)

A Bachelor degree or Licence or Kar-shenasi, normally requiring four years of full time study from the following institutions are assessed as occupying Category B Level 7 of the Qualifications Table and will therefore be awarded 10 points.

Other qualifications from these institutions require NZQA assessment.

  • Amir Kabir University of Technology formerly Tehran Polytechnic
  • Ferdowsi University of Mashhad formerly University of Mashhad and Ferdowsi University
  • Shahid {Martyr}Beheshti University formerly National {Melli} University of Iran
  • Shahid {Martyr} Chamran University formally Jundi/Gondi Shapur University and Ahwaz University
  • Sharif University of Technology/Sharif Industrial University formally Arya Mehr University of Technology
  • Shiraz University/University of Shiraz formerly Pahlavi University
  • University of Esfahan {Isfahan}
  • University of Tabriz formerly Azarabadegan University
  • University of Tehran{Teheran}

Effective 04/02/2002
